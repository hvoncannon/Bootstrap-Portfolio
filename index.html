About Me

Hello, my name is Hayden.

I am originally from Laguna Niguel, California. I still live here today!

After high school, I moved to Santa Barbara for a year and lived in Isla Vista while attending Santa Barbara City College.

After a year, I decided to move to San Francisco. I live there for 5 years and worked as a meat and fish cutter for Whole Foods. I moved back home to Laguna Niguel a few years ago, and have been working an IT job at an office since moving back.

I am very excited to be in the bootcamp, and can not wait until we get into Javascript. My goal is to move into a web development job after completing the program. In my free time I enjoy bad movies, video games, and IPAs.
